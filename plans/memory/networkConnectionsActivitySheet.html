Network Connections Activity Sheet

Learners are to complete the following:

Pairs have 15 minutes to identify interesting information that emerges from consideration the TCP Connections output and Open Sockets output related to the outcome of the previous block of activities on processes. The instructor will issue a ONE minute warning when time is nearly elapsed.
